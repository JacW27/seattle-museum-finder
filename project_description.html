Project Description

The Seattle Museum Finder is a multi-page web application, including Project Description, Museum Map, Team pages. The intention of this web app is to help UW students, vistors, and all museum-lovers to discover major museums across Seattle and the Puget Sound area in a more convenient and straightforward way.

Goals

  • Show locations of major museums near UW and across Seattle.
  • Provide basic information such as name, address, etc.
  • Reveal which museums are closest to UW using distance-based tools.
  • Display nearby public parking garages to support trip planning.

Data

We use GeoJSON files for both museum locations and public parking garages. For the museums, we created a custom GeoJSON dataset in geojson.io manually based on information pulled from the Visit Seattle website - including each museum's name, address, etc. For parking, we began with the City of Seattle's public garages and lots dataset and used a script with GeoPandas and Shapely to retain only garages and lots within a quarter-mile walking distance of each museum before exporting the result to a separate GeoJSON layer for our map.

Targeted Audience

Our main users are UW students, visitors, and general museum-lovers who want a quick overview of local museums and check nearby parking options.
